About Me

Hi, my name is Husein Harun and I am a graduate student in Biological systems engineering at UNL. My research focuses on developing sustainable agriculture practices that reduce environmental impact while increasing crop yields.

I have always been interested in the intersection of biology and engineering, and I am excited to apply my knowledge and skills to real-world problems. Outside of my academic work, I enjoy hiking, reading, and volunteering at a local food bank.
